Who am I?

Hi. I'm Kyle, and I am a:

  • Student
  • Musician
  • Teacher
  • Basketball Fan

But foremost, I am a brother and a son in a family of five.

I was born in South Korea on December 20th, 1997. I moved to Calgary with my family in 2002 at the age of 5 and I have lived in beloved Cowtown since then. I graduated from Western Canada High School in 2015 as the first graduating class of the Redhawks and currently attend the University of Calgary as a 2nd year Chemistry student. You can find me watching basketball, working, and on the occasion, studying!

I currently work as a part-time Music Theory Instructor at Canatabile Music School and as a Regional Director with Sustainable Youth Canada. I volunteer with Distress Centre Calgary, and I hold various executive positions on the UofC campus in clubs and initatives!
